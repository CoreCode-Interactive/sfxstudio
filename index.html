Project Title.

A lightweight web-based image and video projection, as well as audio, cue software. All you need is a free Supabase account, and this site will allow you to upload media to your backend, fetch media from the backend, create a list of cues, and fire them on a separate window, all for free. Intended for use on a computer with a secondary monitor hooked up and extended displays.
